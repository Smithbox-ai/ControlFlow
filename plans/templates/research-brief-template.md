# Research Brief Template

Use this as a compact handoff from Researcher to Planner and downstream executors.

## Research Brief: {Question}

**Artifact Type:** ResearchBrief
**schema_version:** 1.0.0
**Trace ID:** optional UUID

### Summary

- Three to five high-signal bullets only.

### Top Files

- `path` - why this file matters; include symbols when useful.

### Key Findings

- Finding with evidence path and line numbers.

### Ranked Options

- Rank, option, trade-offs, confidence.

### Uncertainties

- Remaining unknowns and why they matter.

### Do Not Re-read

- Already-inspected paths downstream agents should skip unless new evidence contradicts the brief.
